# CV Project Selection

Use four projects on a one-page CV. Add a fifth only when space permits.

## Data Science Version

### 1. Urban Mobility & Mode Structure Analysis

- Built a source-traceable mobility panel covering 242 city-year records, 22 cities/counties, and 11 official survey years from 2009-2024.
- Implemented PCA, K-means++, silhouette scoring, and adjusted Rand stability testing; selected a two-cluster solution with silhouette = 0.727 and mean seed ARI = 0.951.

### 2. Global Maritime Network & Cargo Flow Analysis

- Built a reproducible UNCTAD pipeline covering 182 connectivity economies, 95 container-throughput economies, and 1,785 economy-cargo records.
- Validated the LSCI-log throughput relationship using 5,000 bootstrap samples, obtaining a 95% correlation interval of 0.791-0.882.

### 3. Freeway VD & ETC Traffic Flow Analysis

- Integrated 720 VD minute records and 1,440 ETC vehicle-type records into 144 road-section-period observations with 100% merge completeness.
- Evaluated congestion classifiers across five thresholds, measuring global agreement of 79.17%-88.89% and Cohen's kappa of 0.405-0.611.

### 4. Speed-Flow Relationship Analysis on Freeways

- Built a pipeline to download and parse 120 official XML snapshots into weighted minute-level traffic observations.
- Quantified a 104.67% increase in mean flow and a 29.64 km/h speed decline, identifying 33 congested minutes and a 32-minute continuous event.

## Supply Chain Management Version

### 1. Red Sea Port Resilience Analysis

- Reconstructed a before/after shipping-network dataset covering 27 labelled port-route impacts across 22 ports.
- Quantified the rerouting of 25 of 26 services, deployment of 53 additional vessels, and 85.24% transshipment share of gross labelled capacity impacts.

### 2. Global Maritime Network & Cargo Flow Analysis

- Modelled maritime connectivity and container throughput across 95 economies and developed hub, volatility, cargo-concentration, and trade-role rankings.
- Tested model robustness after excluding up to the top 10 throughput economies; log-linear R-squared remained within 0.682-0.739.

### 3. Urban Mobility & Mode Structure Analysis

- Benchmarked mobility structure across 22 cities/counties and quantified Taipei's public-transport recovery from 37.2% in 2022 to 38.3% in 2024.
- Developed stable city mobility segments using PCA and K-means to support differentiated transport-policy planning.

### 4. Freeway VD & ETC Traffic Flow Analysis

- Integrated cross-source traffic indicators and developed threshold-sensitivity and anomaly-ranking workflows for operational monitoring.
